Internal Auditor

Internal audit is conducted by the business house to reduce the risk of fraud and have additional check on the business transactions. This help business houses to ensure control over business and save money from any leakages or theft. This will boost the governance level within your business and help you to have control over business as you grow. We cover following in internal audit

  • Setting up internal audit function
  • Assessment of internal control
  • Stock audits
  • Fixed asset verifications
  • Review of all financial transactions
